3_BOA_map_visualization.load_files()

Loads multiple shapefiles and geopackage files containing geographical and census data.

Returns: boa_2018, boa_2019, boa_2020, boa_2021, boa_2022 (geopandas.GeoDataFrame): The loaded shapefile data for different years. gpkg_data (geopandas.GeoDataFrame): The loaded geopackage data. cbs (geopandas.GeoDataFrame): The loaded census data.

Note: The column ‘cbs_grid_code’ in ‘cbs’ is renamed to ‘CBS_code’ in order to merge with CBS data.

3_BOA_map_visualization.merge_data(_boa_2018, _boa_2019, _boa_2020, _boa_2021, _boa_2022, _cbs)

Merges shapefile data with census data for different years.

Args: _boa_2018, _boa_2019, _boa_2020, _boa_2021, _boa_2022 (geopandas.GeoDataFrame): Shapefile data for different years. _cbs (geopandas.GeoDataFrame): Census data.

Returns: merged_data_2018, merged_data_2019, merged_data_2020, merged_data_2021, merged_data_2022 (geopandas.GeoDataFrame): The merged data for different years.

Note: The merge is performed based on the ‘CBS_code’ column.
